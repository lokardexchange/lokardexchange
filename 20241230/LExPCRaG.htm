Class Rules and Guidelines

NFC's are one of the concerns of Forensic. Unless you are expecting an emergency, please keep your phones inside your bag.
During online meetings, your camera must be turned on, and a microphone should be connected and ready in case you are called for recitation, attendance, or other class interactions.
Raise your hand if you want to add or say something. You may eat or sleep as you like but do not disturb the class by making unnecessary noise.
A CIA Director and deputy shall be elected once the classlist is finalized. They are responsible for forwarding class concerns to me, vice versa, and for keeping class in order.
Bring a pen and a quarter of yellow paper. Expect a quiz every meeting.
Cheating will not be tolerated. If caught, I will crumple the culprit's paper and bring them to the disciplinary officer.
Do not horse around. It is for our safety and for the preservation of the integrity of the evidence.
While we will tackle some tools and methods, I will just review the needed foundation. Since we are also bound to follow the syllabus, we cannot learn it all.
When I say Restart, it is different from Shutdown and Start. We may also be performing investigations on volatile systems or transient pieces of evidence, where procedures should be followed in detail. Also be alert and be mindful of non-verbal signals.
Group coherence and dynamics are every member's duty. Once grouped, please cooperate and participate. There will be a group evaluation.
Follow standards in nomenclature, recording, and use of units.
Laptops and tablets may sometimes be allowed for class-related research, but not for unrelated activities. Mute all notifications.
Keep your workspace tidy and dispose of waste properly. Handle classroom equipment with care.
Familiarize yourself with emergency procedures and adhere to safety protocols. Be mindful of health guidelines if applicable.
Share constructive feedback with the instructor and stay curious by seeking additional resources to deepen your understanding.
You should have a Discord account and an ID photo for Recitation Cards.
