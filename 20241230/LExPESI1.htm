Emergency Safety Instructions

What to do during an Earthquake:

  • Drop to your hands and knees.
  • Cover your head and neck with your arms.
  • Take cover under a sturdy table or against an interior wall if possible.
  • Hold on until the shaking stops.
  • Avoid windows, heavy objects, or anything that may fall.

What to do during a Fire:

  • Alert others and evacuate immediately.
  • Stay low to avoid smoke inhalation.
  • Use the nearest exit, not the elevator.
  • If clothing catches fire, stop, drop, and roll.
  • Call emergency services once safe.

What to do during a Terrorism Threat:

  • Stay calm and listen for instructions from authorities.
  • Evacuate the building if possible, but avoid rushing to exits.
  • If you are trapped, find a safe place and stay quiet.
  • Keep a low profile and avoid windows or doors.
  • Call authorities when it is safe to do so.
